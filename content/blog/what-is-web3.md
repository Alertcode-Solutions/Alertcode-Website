Web3 represents a decentralized model for building and operating internet applications.

Instead of relying on a single platform owner, systems can use blockchain infrastructure to coordinate trust, ownership, and transparency across participants.

At Alertcode, Web3 is less about hype and more about **engineering outcomes**: verifiable data flows, resilient digital identity, and programmable trust layers.

## Why this matters

- It reduces dependence on centralized intermediaries.
- It enables auditable transaction histories.
- It opens new product and business models.

## Practical applications

Teams can apply Web3 selectively in areas like settlements, credential verification, and interoperability between services.

Strong architecture still matters most: clear domain boundaries, secure integration points, and measurable business value.
